The Easiest Way to Play Walk-Up Songs at a Baseball Game

Walk-up music turns a routine at-bat into a moment. The hitter's name booming through the speaker, their favorite song kicking in as they step to the plate — it's how Major League players get hyped, and now any youth baseball or softball team can do the same. OnDeckDJ is the iOS app that makes it ridiculously easy to set up walk-up music for every player on your roster, then run it from your phone with one-tap controls during the game.

Built for coaches, parents, and team DJs, OnDeckDJ pairs each batter's chosen song with an AI-generated stadium announcer voice that calls them to the plate by name and number — no recording studio, no audio engineer, no setup hassle. Just add your players, pick their walk-up song from Apple Music or your own MP3 collection, and tap play.
